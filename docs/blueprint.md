# **App Name**: Médard Ligne Commerce

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse a comprehensive catalog of Médard Ligne products with high-quality images and clear pricing.
- Product Details View: View detailed information for each product, including descriptions, multiple images, and available variations.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add, remove, and update quantities of Médard Ligne items in a persistent shopping cart.
- Secure Checkout Process: A streamlined and secure multi-step checkout flow for payment and shipping information to complete orders.
- User Account & Order History: Users can create an account, log in, manage their profile, and view their past Médard Ligne orders and their status.
- AI Product Recommender: An AI tool that suggests relevant Médard Ligne products to users based on their browsing history or popular items.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design will adopt a dark color scheme to evoke a sense of modern elegance and sophistication, aligning with a premium 'ligne' (line) of products.
- Primary color: A sophisticated lavender-purple (#A894D1). This provides a rich and calm, yet modern, feel, offering excellent contrast against the dark background.
- Background color: A very dark desaturated indigo (#241F2E). This deep, almost black, background serves to highlight product imagery and textual content, creating an immersive experience.
- Accent color: A vibrant royal blue (#5269E0). This striking hue will be used sparingly for interactive elements, calls to action, and subtle highlights, drawing the user's attention without overwhelming the serene palette.
- Headlines: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant and high-end feel, reflecting the product's quality. Body text: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and a contemporary touch.
- Clean, minimalist, and line-based icons will be utilized throughout the application to complement the sleek and modern aesthetic of Médard Ligne.
- A spacious and uncluttered grid-based layout will showcase Médard Ligne products effectively, emphasizing visual content and ensuring intuitive navigation.
- Subtle and smooth animations will enhance user interactions, such as product image transitions and additions to the shopping cart, contributing to a refined user experience.
